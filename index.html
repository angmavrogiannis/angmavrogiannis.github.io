About Me

I am a second year master's student in the department of Mechanical Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. My interests lie in the fields of robotics and machine learning. I am currently working on vehicle behavior prediction, advised by Prof. Changliu Liu at the Intelligent Control Lab in the Robotics Institute. In the past, I received my diploma in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Patras (Greece). For my diploma thesis, I worked with Prof. Argyris Dentsoras on developing a framework for solving optimization problems using a parser and genetic algorithms.
