About Me

I am a second-year master's student in the department of Mechanical Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. I am broadly interested in the development of planning, estimation and learning algorithms applied in robotics. I am a member of Intelligent Control Lab in the Robotics Institute and my thesis is focused on vehicle behavior prediction, advised by Prof. Changliu Liu. Prior to that, I received my diploma in Mechanical Engineering and Aeronautics from the University of Patras (Greece). For my diploma thesis, I worked with Prof. Argyris Dentsoras on the development of a framework for automatic parsing and solution of optimization problems formulated in structured language.
